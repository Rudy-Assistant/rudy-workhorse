WHAT IT DOES
================================================================================

RESEARCH FEED SYSTEM WORKFLOW:

Input:  20+ RSS feeds (AI/ML, legal tech, privacy, automation)
        ↓
Fetch:  Parallel feed fetching with 10s timeouts per feed
        ↓
Parse:  Extract title, URL, summary, publish date
        ↓
Score:  Keyword matching + category multipliers (AI: 2.5x, Privacy: 1.5x, etc.)
        ↓
Dedup:  Compare against historical cache.json to skip duplicates
        ↓
Rank:   Sort by relevance score (high → low)
        ↓
Output: JSON report (detailed, structured)
        Markdown digest (human-readable, categorized)
        ↓
Store:  C:\Users\C\Desktop\rudy-logs\research-feed-[date].json
        C:\Users\C\Desktop\rudy-logs\research-digest-[date].md
